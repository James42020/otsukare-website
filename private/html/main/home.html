Tutorial

Firstly if you want to return to this home page at any time, press the home button ( ) in the navbar, or click on the brand logo in the top-left.

To look at our articles you can press the search button ( ) to look at all of the articles and easily search for specific articles you want to see.

If you don't know what you want to look at, try the random button ( ) which will pick an article at random for you to look at and learn.

Found some good articles? At the top of each article you can press on "Add to Favourites" to add an article into your Favourites list for easy access, if you would like to remove an article from your Favourites, simpily go on the article you wish to remove and click "Remove from Favourites" to remove it from your Favourites list.

You can find all of your Favourites by clicking the star button ( ) in the navbar. (Note: Favourites are stored in your browser data and clearing it will remove all of your Favourites.)

If you wish to share an article or view it on another device, you can press the QR Code button ( ) to get a QR Code of the article/page you are currently on.

Prefer dark mode? Click on the dark/light mode button ( ) to toggle between dark mode and light mode.

And Finally, you can click on the links button ( ) to view links to all of the resources I have used to learn Japanese, I would recommending checking it out to see if there are any resources that might be useful to you.
